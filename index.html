About AIMAK

Leading Aeronautical Information Management in Kenya

Our Mission

To foster professional development, uphold the highest standards of aeronautical data quality, and represent the interests of Kenya's AIM community at national and international levels.

Our Vision

To be the leading professional association for aeronautical information management in East Africa, recognized for excellence, innovation, and impact in advancing aviation safety and efficiency.

Our Core Focus Areas

AIMAK exists to support and elevate the professionals who deliver essential aeronautical information services.

Aeronautical Information Services (AIS)

We champion the integrity and digital advancement of core aeronautical information services. Our advocacy focuses on upholding ICAO Annex 15 standards and guiding the essential transition from traditional services to a modern, data-centric environment.

NOTAM Origination & Management

We focus on the processes and expertise required for efficient origination, quality control, and dissemination of NOTAMs, which are vital for real-time flight safety. Our forums address challenges in improving clarity and reducing ambiguity.

Aeronautical Charting & Procedure Design

We champion the skills behind designing and producing precise visual and digital aeronautical charts, as well as instrument flight procedures essential for safe navigation.

Professional Standards & Training

We are committed to continuous growth of our members. AIMAK facilitates access to specialized training, workshops on emerging technologies, and capacity-building opportunities aligned with global AIM roadmaps.

Industry Representation & Consultancy

AIMAK serves as the unified voice for AIM professionals, engaging with regulators like KCAA and international bodies. We provide expert consultation on national and regional AIM improvement initiatives.

Events & Activities

Collaboration, Learning, and Community Impact

Why We Gather

At AIMAK, we value collaboration, learning, and giving back to the community. Our events bring together aviation professionals, stakeholders, and members to exchange knowledge, strengthen skills, and support community-focused initiatives.

Through these activities, we reinforce our commitment to safety, service, and the growth of the aeronautical information profession.

Knowledge Exchange
Community Impact

Annual General Meetings

AIMAK's Annual General Meeting brings together AIM professionals and invited aviation professionals from across Kenya and neighboring countries. The AGM provides an opportunity to review the year's progress, elect leaders, discuss key issues, and shape the association's future direction.

It also serves as a valuable networking platform for members, regulators, and industry stakeholders. AGMs are held at a central location, with remote participation options and advance notices shared through email and the AIMAK website.

Corporate Social Responsibility & Community Outreach

AIMAK recognizes that aviation and the safety it depends on is part of a broader social responsibility. Through CSR and outreach, we aim to give back to our community while promoting aviation awareness and education.

Aviation Education Outreach

Organizing seminars or visits to schools, universities, and technical colleges to introduce young people to careers in aviation.

Community Safety Awareness

Collaborating with partners to raise awareness about airport- and airspace-related safety among communities neighboring airports.

Support Programs

Collaborating with well-wishers to donate resources, sponsor educational materials, or support youth programmes in underserved communities.

Environmental Sustainability

Tree planting initiatives and environmental conservation efforts as part of our commitment to sustainable aviation.
