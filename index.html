About AIMAK

Leading Aeronautical Information Management in Kenya

Our Mission

To advance the standards and practices of aeronautical information management in Kenya through professional excellence, continuous training, and international collaboration.

Our Vision

To be the leading professional association for aeronautical information management in East Africa.

What We Do

Professional Development

We promote continuous training and capacity building programs for AIM professionals to maintain international standards and best practices.

Standards & Quality

We promote adherence to ICAO standards and ensure quality assurance in aeronautical information management across Kenya.

Networking & Collaboration

We facilitate collaboration among aviation stakeholders, regulatory bodies, and international organizations through IFAIMA membership.

Advocacy & Representation

We represent the interests of AIM professionals and advocate for improved aeronautical information management infrastructure.

Understanding Sector Flight Plans

What is a Sector Flight Plan?

A sector flight plan is a flight plan filed at the aerodrome of departure but intended for the sectors immediately following the point of arrival. This ensures seamless coordination and safety across multiple flight segments.
